**Chef Mike's amazing guacamole :-)**

![Bowl of guac](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/ed/Guacamole_avocados.jpg)

* **

_Ingredients_

   - 2 avocados
   - 1 lime
   -  2 tsp salt
   - jalapeño to taste
   - tomato
   - onion

* **
*Instructions*

   - cut and mash avocados
   - finely chop onion
   - chop the jalapeño
   - chop the tomato
   - squeeze lime
   - add salt
   - and mix well
   - add tomato and jalapeno
